Join 40,000+ players in experiencing Terraria's sweetest mod! 

Bombus Apis introduces a completely custom, fully fleshed-out new combat class: The Beekeeper. Control the swarm, manage a brand new resource, and utilize high-damage homing weaponry to melt bosses. 

Using the beekeeper's Loyal Bees, you can command an army of bees with three modes, able to be swapped in combat: 
[list]
[*] [b]Defense:[/b] Grants defensive bonuses and a honey shield.
[*] [b]Offense:[/b] Loyal Bees seek out and attack enemies.
[*] [b]Gathering:[/b] Bees lodge into enemies for DoT and increased honey regen.
[/list]

Leverage the power of the hive to become Terraria's greatest beekeeper!

This mod currently has 75 weapons, 7 armor sets, and 35 accessories. 

This includes 11 weapons, and 7 accessories that are cross-mod specifically with the Calamity Mod.
